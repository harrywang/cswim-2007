CSWIM 2007 Author Guidelines

Submission Instructions

All submissions must be in English, and the file must be submitted in Microsoft Word .DOC or PDF via the workshop system at http://cswim2007.ecom.arizona.edu/system.

However, the camera-ready copy of accepted manuscripts MUST be submitted in the MS Word format in order to facilitate the creation of CSWIM online proceedings. Length of submissions, including all contents, is limited to 5 single-spaced, 8.5” by 11” pages with Times New Roman, 12-point font, and 1” margins on all sides. Violation of the format will result in the rejection of the submission without review.

>> Template (.doc) for initial submission (without author information)

>> Template (.doc) for final accepted papers

All submissions will be reviewed double blind. Selection for presentation at the workshop will be based on originality, contribution to the field, and workshop interest. An important criterion in evaluating the submission is the preference for a problem-driven approach, i.e., papers that focus on pure technical issues but are not clearly relevant to solving business problems are less likely to be accepted. Extended abstracts of presentations will be published at the workshop website.

Early registration closes: June 15, 2007

Workshop dates: July 22-23, 2007
